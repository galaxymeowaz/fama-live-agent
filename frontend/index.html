Trust & ROI Shield

We know that uploading photos of your home or store is a big deal. Many people worry that their pictures might be shared, sold, or used to teach an AI something — without their knowledge or consent. We built Fama with that concern front and centre.

Your photos stay on your device: By default, everything is saved using browser LocalStorage — think of it like a private notebook that only your browser (and only you) can read. It never leaves your phone or computer unless you choose to. Cloud backup is only available if you opt into it with Diamond Tier.
We will never use your photos to train AI: When you upload a picture, it goes straight into a one-time analysis. Once Fama is done, it is deleted from memory. Your images are never stored on our servers, never seen by our team, and never fed into any AI model — not ours, not anyone else's.
Real results, not ads: Our product suggestions for businesses are based on data-driven layout science — not hidden sponsorships or advertising deals. What we recommend, we genuinely believe will help your store perform better.
